Before you reach out

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → find TripIQ → Cancel. We can't cancel subscriptions on your behalf — they're managed through Google Play.
The analysis shows wrong numbers. What do I do?
Check your expense profile first — make sure your fuel price, consumption, and platform fee are set correctly for your currency and region. If the extraction itself is wrong (wrong platform or pay amount detected), email us the screenshot and we'll investigate.
My platform isn't recognized. Will you add it?
TripIQ currently supports Uber, Lyft, Grab, DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Foodpanda. It will also attempt to analyze screenshots from unrecognized platforms. Send us the platform name and your country and we'll prioritize based on demand.
I lost my trip history. Can you recover it?
Trip history is stored on your device only — we don't have access to it. If you cleared the App's data, uninstalled, or switched phones, the data is gone. This is by design for your privacy.
How fast do you respond?
We're a small team. Expect a reply within 24–48 hours on business days. Bug reports and billing issues get priority.
