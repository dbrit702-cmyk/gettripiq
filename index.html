Common questions

Things drivers ask before installing.

Does TripIQ read my screen or run in the background?

No. It uses screenshots only. No accessibility permissions, no screen overlay, no background services. Take a screenshot, share it to the app, get a verdict. Done.

Does it support my country and currency?

10 currencies are supported: PHP, USD, AUD, CAD, GBP, SGD, MYR, THB, IDR, TWD. The breakeven math, currency symbol, and unit (km or miles) all adapt to what you select.

How accurate is the AI extraction?

The AI reads platform, gross pay, distance, and estimated time from your screenshot. If a reading looks low-confidence, it automatically retries with a stronger model. You'll see exactly what was extracted on the verdict screen.

Is my trip data sent anywhere?

Screenshots are sent to our analysis service to extract the offer data, then discarded. Your trip history is saved on your phone only — no account, no cloud sync.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro is a Google Play subscription managed through your Play Store account. Cancel any time, no commitment.

What if my platform isn't on the supported list?

TripIQ will still try to extract gross pay, distance, and time from any trip-offer screenshot, and label the platform "Unknown". Send us feedback and we'll prioritize new platforms based on demand.
